Dropdowns

Dropdowns are toggleable, contextual overlays for displaying lists of links and more. They’re made interactive with the included dropdown directives.

Examples

Wrap the dropdown’s toggle (your button or link) and the dropdown menu within dropdown. Dropdowns can be triggered from a tag or button tag elements to better fit your potential needs.
